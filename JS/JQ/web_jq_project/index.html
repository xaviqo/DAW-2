Signal is a legendary music store located in the heart of Barcelona. Founded in 1978 by a group of music enthusiasts, the store has been a meeting place for musicians, fans, and collectors for over four decades.In its early years, Signal focused on selling rock and punk vinyl and cassettes imported from around the world.
Signal continues to be a reference in Barcelona's music scene. The store is still owned by its original founders and is run by a second generation of employees, many of whom have worked there for decades. At Signal, music remains the soul of everything we do.
